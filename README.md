
#  Marketing Campaign Analysis – Power BI Dashboard

##  Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end **Marketing Campaign Analysis Dashboard** built using **Power BI**, focused on tracking performance, ROI, CPA, CPC and revenue impact across multiple digital marketing channels.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly identify high-performing campaigns, optimize ad spend, and improve conversion efficiency.

---

## Objectives

* Monitor overall marketing performance across channels
* Analyze spend vs revenue trends over time
* Identify top-performing and under-performing campaigns
* Evaluate KPIs like **CTR, CPC, CPA, ROI, ROAS & Conversion Rate**

---

## Key KPIs Tracked

| Metric                | Description                      |
| --------------------- | -------------------------------- |
| **Total Clicks**      | Total number of ad clicks        |
| **Total Revenue**     | Revenue generated from campaigns |
| **Total Ad Spend**    | Total marketing expenditure      |
| **Total Conversions** | Successful conversions achieved  |
| **Conversion Rate %** | % of clicks converted            |
| **ROAS**              | Return on Ad Spend               |
| **CPA**               | Cost per Acquisition             |
| **Average ROI**       | Overall return on investment     |
| **CPC**               | Cost per Click                   |
| **CTR %**             | Click Through Rate               |

---

##  Dashboard Features

* **Spend by Marketing Channel** – Donut chart showing channel-wise budget distribution
* **Spend & Conversions Trend** – Monthly trend analysis
* **Impressions vs Clicks** – Performance comparison by month
* **Revenue vs Ad Spend** – Side-by-side monthly performance
* **Revenue by Marketing Channel** – Channel-wise revenue contribution
* **Interactive Filters** – Channel slicer & Campaign Date range filter
* **Campaign Level Table** – CTR, CPC, ROI & Revenue for each campaign

---

##  Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI Desktop**
* **Power Query** – Data cleaning & transformation
* **DAX** – KPI calculations
* **Excel / CSV Dataset**

---

##  Dataset

The dataset contains campaign-level data including:

* Campaign ID
* Marketing Channel
* Clicks, Impressions
* Ad Spend
* Revenue
* CTR, CPC, ROI, Conversions
* Campaign Date (Month & Year)

---

##  Business Insights

* Google Ads and Referral channels contribute the **highest revenue**
* Instagram Ads show **high CTR but moderate ROI**
* Influencer Marketing has **lower CPC but inconsistent conversions**
* Email Campaigns require optimization due to **lower revenue share**
